Instant Ramen Noodles

a very nice instant ramen

the best food chosen by all happy college students

Ingredients

  • water
  • instant cup ramen

Recipe Instructions

  1. boil the water in a kettle or anything available
  2. open the top film halfway and put the seasoning in
  3. put the boiling water in the cup ramen
  4. wait for 5 minutes or the specified time required for different noodles
  5. hide the pain and enjoy
